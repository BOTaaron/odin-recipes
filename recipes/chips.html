Potato Chips Recipe

A Traditional British Chip

A recipe for traditional British chips. A healthier variant, cooked in the oven rather than fried. These will be less greasy and more seasoned for flavour. They use Maris Piper potatoes for that extra crisp.

  • 1kg Maris Piper Potatoes
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Your favourite herbs/spices
  1. Optionally peel the potatoes. This is not necessary as the skin is obviously edible but they will look better.
  2. Cut the potatoes into slices, and then into chip shapes. Place into a pot of water with salt.
  3. Gently boil the chips until they soften but don't fall apart. Strain off the water and let them cool a little on a flat tray so the water evaporates.
  4. Once dry, massage with oil and sprinkle over some salt, pepper, and any herbs or spices desired.
  5. Cook the chips in the oven for around 30 minutes at 200C, or until they have a nice crispy brown appearance.
